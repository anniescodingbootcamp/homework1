About Me

I am currently a student at UCLA coding bootcamp. I find coding computers very interesting.

I went to Chapman University for undergraduate where I studied business with an emphasis in Advertising. I was in at University from 2001 through 2005. I then went on to work in the Advertising field. I worked for a large Advertising Agency called Heil-Brice Retail Advertising. After doing that for several years I decided to start my own high end clothing line. This was extremely challenging.

Starting my own business was very challenging. I had to do all the work myself and I hired an assistant designer. After owning my own business I retired because I had made some very good investments that I could live off of. I am now interested in learning coding so I can build my own app. The business for computer programmers is booming. Instead of hiring a company I decided to learn coding so I could do it myself.
